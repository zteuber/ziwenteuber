Unpacking Parental Involvement Challenging Achievement Situations

Project Design

The UPICAS project investigates parental involvement under challenging circumstances using a mixed-methods approach. It combines surveys, interviews, and observational studies to understand how parents support their children in complex educational and social situations.

Aims

  • Examine the forms and strategies of parental involvement in difficult contexts.
  • Identify factors that facilitate or hinder effective parental support.
  • Provide recommendations for educators and policymakers to better engage families.

Status

The project is currently in the data collection phase, with preliminary analysis underway. Findings are expected to inform both academic research and practical guidance for parents and schools.
